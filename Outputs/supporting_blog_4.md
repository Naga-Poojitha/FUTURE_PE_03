# Workflow Automation for SMEs – A Practical Guide

Workflow automation connects your business tools into one structured system.

## What Can Be Automated?

- Enquiry → Follow-up → Invoice process
- Customer onboarding
- Internal approvals
- Reporting dashboards

## Benefits

- Reduced errors
- Faster processing
- Clear accountability
- Scalable operations

Workflow automation helps SMEs grow without increasing workload.
