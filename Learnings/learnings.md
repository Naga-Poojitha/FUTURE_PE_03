This project demonstrates how structured prompt engineering can be used to generate SEO-optimized content clusters for local businesses.

Business Used:

- Supporting blog cluster

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- SEO content planning
- Keyword intent mapping
- Content clustering strategy
- Local SEO integration
- Prompt engineering for long-form content

---

## Outcome

Created a complete SEO Content Pack ready for publishing on a local business website to improve rankings and generate organic leads.
